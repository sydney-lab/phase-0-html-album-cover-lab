Happy

by Pharrell Williams

“Happy” is a feel-good anthem from Pharrell Williams that swept the globe with its upbeat melody and joyful lyrics.

Originally written for the film Despicable Me 2, this song became an international hit and earned multiple award nominations.

The music video for “Happy” featured people from around the world dancing in the streets, showing the universal power of joy.
